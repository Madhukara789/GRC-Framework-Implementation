# Risk Treatment Plan

This document outlines the actions taken to address identified information security risks.

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Risk Level | Treatment Option | Mitigation Action | Risk Owner | Target Date | Status |
|--------|-----------------|-----------|------------------|------------------|------------|-------------|--------|
| R-01 | Unauthorized access to customer data | High | Mitigate | Implement MFA and role-based access controls | IT Security | 30 days | Planned |
| R-02 | Cloud misconfiguration leading to data exposure | High | Mitigate | Conduct cloud security configuration reviews | Cloud Team | 45 days | Planned |
| R-03 | Malware infection via phishing emails | High | Mitigate | Conduct security awareness training | IT Operations | 60 days | Planned |
| R-04 | Delayed incident detection | Medium | Mitigate | Improve monitoring and alerting | Security Team | 45 days | Planned |
| R-05 | Low-impact operational risk | Low | Accept | Risk accepted by management | Management | N/A | Accepted |
