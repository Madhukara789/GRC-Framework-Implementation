# Internal Audit Checklist

This checklist is used to assess the implementation of key information security controls.

| Control Area | Control Requirement | Evidence Reviewed | Status |
|-------------|--------------------|------------------|--------|
| Information Security Policy | Policy documented and approved | Policy document | Compliant |
| Asset Management | Asset inventory maintained | Asset Register | Compliant |
| Access Control | User access managed and reviewed | Access Control Policy | Partial |
| Incident Management | Incident response process defined | Incident Response Policy | Compliant |
| Data Protection | GDPR requirements addressed | Data Protection Policy | Partial |
| Risk Assessment | Risks identified and documented | Risk Register | Compliant |
