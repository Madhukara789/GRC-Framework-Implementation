# Risk Assessment

This section documents the risk assessment process for FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd, including risk identification, risk analysis, and risk evaluation aligned with ISO/IEC 27001.

# Risk Assessment

## Objective
The objective of this risk assessment is to identify, analyze, and evaluate information security risks that may impact the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets at FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd.

## Scope
The risk assessment covers:
- Information assets listed in the Asset Inventory
- Cloud and on-premise infrastructure
- Applications, databases, and user access
- Regulatory and compliance requirements (ISO 27001, GDPR, SOC 2)

## Risk Assessment Methodology
The organization follows a qualitative risk assessment approach aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:

- Risks are identified based on threats and vulnerabilities affecting assets
- Likelihood and impact are rated as Low, Medium, or High
- Risk level is determined based on likelihood Ã— impact
- Risks are prioritized for treatment based on their severity

## Risk Criteria
- **Likelihood**: Probability of the risk occurring
- **Impact**: Business, financial, operational, or regulatory impact
- **Risk Level**: Overall risk rating used for decision-making

## Output
The outcome of this risk assessment is a documented Risk Register that forms the basis for risk treatment and control selection.
