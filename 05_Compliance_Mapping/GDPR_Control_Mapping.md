# GDPR Control Mapping

This document maps key GDPR requirements to organizational policies and controls implemented at FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd.

| GDPR Article | Requirement | Related Policy / Control |
|-------------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Article 5 | Principles of processing personal data | Data Protection and Privacy Policy |
| Article 6 | Lawfulness of processing | Data Protection and Privacy Policy |
| Article 24 | Responsibility of the controller | Information Security Policy |
| Article 25 | Data protection by design and default | Risk Assessment, Security Controls |
| Article 30 | Records of processing activities | Asset Inventory |
| Article 32 | Security of processing | Access Control Policy, Risk Treatment |
| Article 33 | Personal data breach notification | Incident Response Policy |

## Notes
This mapping highlights how GDPR requirements are addressed through documented policies and risk-based controls.
