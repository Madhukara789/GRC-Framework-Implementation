# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
To define acceptable and unacceptable use of organizational information systems and resources.

## Scope
This policy applies to all users accessing FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd systems and networks.

## Acceptable Use
- Systems shall be used for authorized business purposes only
- Users shall follow security best practices
- Confidential information shall not be disclosed without authorization

## Prohibited Activities
- Sharing user credentials
- Installing unauthorized software
- Accessing malicious or illegal content

## Compliance
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action.
