# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose
To define a structured approach for identifying, reporting, and responding to information security incidents.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties of FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd.

## Policy Statements
- All information security incidents shall be reported immediately
- Incidents shall be assessed, contained, and resolved in a timely manner
- Evidence shall be preserved where required
- Lessons learned shall be documented to prevent recurrence

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Employees: Report suspected incidents
- Security Team: Investigate and respond to incidents
- Management: Approve corrective and preventive actions

## Compliance
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.
