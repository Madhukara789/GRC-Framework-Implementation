# Information Security Policy

## Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish management direction and support for information security in accordance with business requirements and applicable laws and regulations.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who access information assets of FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd.

## Policy Statement
FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets through the implementation of appropriate administrative, technical, and physical controls.

## Responsibilities
- Management is responsible for enforcing this policy
- Employees must comply with security requirements
- The Information Security team is responsible for monitoring compliance

## Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action and legal consequences.
