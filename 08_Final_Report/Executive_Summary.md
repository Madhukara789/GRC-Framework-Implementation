# Executive Summary

## Overview
This GRC project was conducted to design and implement a structured Governance, Risk, and Compliance framework for FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd, a fictional mid-sized FinTech organization.

The objective was to identify information security risks, ensure regulatory compliance, and establish governance mechanisms aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 and GDPR.

## Scope
The assessment covered:
- Organizational context and asset inventory
- Information security risk assessment
- Policy framework and governance controls
- Compliance mapping to ISO 27001 and GDPR
- Internal audit and gap assessment
- Risk treatment planning

## Key Findings
- Core information security policies are defined and documented
- Asset inventory and risk register are maintained
- Several high and medium risks were identified, primarily related to access control, cloud security, and user awareness
- Partial compliance gaps were identified during internal audit

## Risk Posture
- High risks: Managed through defined mitigation plans
- Medium risks: Prioritized for remediation
- Low risks: Accepted based on business impact

Overall risk posture is assessed as **Medium**, with continuous improvement planned.

## Compliance Status
- ISO/IEC 27001: Partially compliant (controls defined, some implementation gaps)
- GDPR: Partially compliant (data protection controls defined, operational maturity required)

## Recommendations
- Formalize periodic access reviews
- Strengthen cloud security monitoring
- Implement regular security awareness training
- Conduct annual internal audits

## Conclusion
The GRC framework provides a strong foundation for managing information security risks and regulatory compliance. With continued monitoring and improvement, FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd can enhance its overall security and compliance posture.
