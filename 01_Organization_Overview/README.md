# Organization Overview

Company Profile
This project is based on a fictional organization named "FinServe Solutions Pvt Ltd", a mid-sized FinTech company providing digital payment and financial services.

- Industry: Financial Technology (FinTech)
- Employee Strength: 250
- Customers: Retail and SME clients
- Geographic Presence: India

# Business Environment
FinServe Solutions processes and stores sensitive customer information including:
- Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
- Financial and transaction data
- Authentication credentials

The organization operates using a hybrid infrastructure consisting of cloud-hosted services and on-premise systems.

# Regulatory & Compliance Scope
The organization is subject to the following regulatory and compliance requirements:
- ISO/IEC 27001 – Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- GDPR – Protection of EU customer personal data
- SOC 2 – Trust Services Criteria (Security & Availability)

# Risk Appetite
The organization follows a "low to medium risk appetite", prioritizing:
- Customer data protection
- Regulatory compliance
- Service availability

# Project Objective
The objective of this GRC project is to:
- Identify and classify organizational assets
- Assess information security risks
- Define security policies and controls
- Map controls to compliance frameworks
- Perform gap assessment and recommend risk treatment plan.

Desclaimer: All information used in this project is fictional and created solely for learning and demonstration purposes.
